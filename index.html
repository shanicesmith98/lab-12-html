A Bunch of Poems

Goat - Haiku

Thundery foreshore
How a nanny, strange goat prowls
watching the chocolate

Friend - Haiku

Magical hillside
An only, springy friend trots
enjoying the view

Otter - Acrostic

Old lurks wallow
Temporary excitements exult
True hysterics ramble
Embroidered daisies relive
Rich flowers ooze
